JusMundi Global Achievements (2022–2025)

Company overview, funding, product launches & 4-year transformation

Company overview & evolution

Founded: 2019 with the mission of “Building the global rule of law is everyone's responsibility”

Core business: Legal tech platform providing AI-powered search engine and research tools for international law and arbitration, serving legal professionals worldwide with access to treaties, awards, jurisprudence, and legal intelligence.

Financial performance & funding

Funding trajectory

  • 2019: €1 million seed funding → Established Paris office
  • Series A: $10 million → Opened New York office
  • 2023: Bridge funding with existing investors (path to profitability)
  • Series B: $22 million → Accelerated growth and AI development
  • Geographic expansion: Singapore office planned

Business model

  • Subscription-based access to legal intelligence database and libraries
  • Jus Connect marketplace connecting legal professionals
  • Enterprise partnerships with major law firms and institutions globally
  • Research4Life partnership providing free access to 80+ Low and Middle Income Countries (LMICs)

Status: On path to profitability with sustained growth since 2023

Major product launches & innovations

2019: Jus Mundi platform launch

Core features:

  • Multilingual search engine for international law and arbitration
  • Conflict Checker for transparency and conflict of interest detection
  • Cite-Map revealing interconnections between legal resources
  • Wiki Notes for collaborative editorial projects
  • Comprehensive legal intelligence database and libraries

Partnerships for content: 2020 International Bar Association (IBA), 2021 International Chamber of Commerce (ICC). Total partnerships: 92 institutional partnerships globally.

2022: Jus Connect launch (March)

Revolutionary professional network:

  • Data-driven platform connecting in-house legal teams with arbitration experts
  • 300% client growth within first year
  • 40,000+ active monthly users globally
  • Client base: Total Energies, WeBuild, Teklas, governments from UK, Japan, Colombia, Saudi Arabia
  • Professional services: Law firms (Hogan Lovells, FCDG, GBS Disputes, WongPartnership), expert firms (Accuracy, Berkeley Research Group)

2023: Jus AI launch – the AI revolution

Breakthrough AI capabilities:

  • First generative AI product: AI Summary for case documents
  • Jus AI Assistant Alpha: Private trial with select partners
  • Jus AI Assistant Beta: Public release with Acceleration Program
  • Proprietary algorithms: Award anonymization, CiteMap legal citation creator
  • Multilingual legal insights, memo drafting, document summarization

Industry leadership: First mover in legal tech with large language models and generative AI for international arbitration.

2025: Jus AI V2 – next-generation AI platform

Launch date: September 2025

Revolutionary capabilities:

  • Scale: Analyzes up to 75,000 documents per minute with structured views
  • Merged search: AI assistant integrated with legacy search engine (filters + AI power)
  • Enhanced reasoning: Deep search with web browsing capabilities
  • Document capacity: Upload up to 10,000 documents per conversation with tabular review
  • Specialized workflows: Timeline generation, document production review, translation, drafting support
  • On-page deployment: AI directly accessible on document pages
  • Transparency: Verifiable citations, step-by-step reasoning, full scope control

Certifications & security: ISO 27001, SOC II, ISO 42001 standards for responsible AI governance.

Geographic & market expansion

Content growth by region (2024)

  • MENA: +40% content growth
  • Africa: +28% content growth
  • North America: +24% content growth

Partnership expansion (2024)

38 new institutional partnerships added: Brazil/LATAM 10, MENA 8, EU/UK 8, APAC 4, North America 4, Africa 4.

Global presence

  • Multinational team with diverse workforce
  • Offices: Paris (HQ), New York, Singapore (planned)
  • Market penetration: International arbitration → expanding to new legal verticals
  • Active users: 40,000+ monthly active users globally

AI-specific achievements & focus

AI strategy evolution

2023: Foundation – Pioneer in legal AI with proprietary algorithms; first to deploy generative AI for arbitration; AI Acceleration Program.

2024: Expansion – Enhanced AI capabilities, increased document processing capacity, improved multilingual support, security certifications (ISO 27001, SOC II).

2025: Transformation – Jus AI V2 with 75K documents/minute processing; deep reasoning and web integration; specialized legal workflows; ISO 42001 (AI management system) certified.

AI impact metrics

  • User productivity: AI-powered research reduces manual work by 60–80%
  • Processing speed: From hours to minutes for complex legal research
  • Accuracy: Verifiable citations with step-by-step reasoning
  • Multilingual: Seamless research across multiple languages

Vision: specialized legal AI

Strategic focus: Trusted, global AI for all legal professionals; vertical specialization; deep domain expertise; new verticals beyond International Law and Arbitration; CMS integration for advanced arbitration workflows.

Key growth metrics (2022–2025)

Platform metrics

  • Database content: Continuous expansion across all regions
  • User base: 40,000+ monthly active users (Jus Connect alone)
  • Partnerships: 92 institutional partnerships globally
  • Document processing: 75,000 docs/minute capability (2025)
  • Geographic diversity: 100+ countries with content and users

Mission impact

  • Access democratization: 80+ LMICs with free research access via Research4Life
  • Partnership growth: 92 total partnerships (38 added in 2024)
  • Content diversity: Increased representation from MENA, Africa, LATAM, APAC

Major strategic achievements

  • 1. AI leadership (2023–2025): First mover in generative AI for international arbitration; proprietary algorithms; ISO 42001; specialized legal AI vs. generalist competitors.
  • 2. Market expansion (2022–2025): Jus Connect 300% growth in first year; 38 new partnerships in 2024; geographic diversification; new legal verticals.
  • 3. Financial sustainability: $22M Series B; bridge funding; path to profitability; mission-aligned revenue (free access for LMICs).
  • 4. Mission-driven leadership: “Société à Mission” status (2023); independent Mission Committee; third-party audited (COFRAC/Aupeam).

Summary: 4-year transformation

2022–2025 represents a period of explosive growth and innovation:

  • $32M+ raised (Series A + Bridge + Series B)
  • 4 major product launches (Jus Mundi, Jus Connect, Jus AI, Jus AI V2)
  • 92 institutional partnerships globally
  • 40,000+ monthly active users
  • 300% growth in Jus Connect clientele
  • 75,000 docs/minute AI processing capability
  • 3 ISO certifications (27001, SOC II, 42001)
  • 80+ LMICs with free research access
  • Mission-driven status with independent governance
  • AI industry leadership in legal tech

JusMundi has evolved from a legal research platform to the world's leading AI-powered legal intelligence platform for international law and arbitration, with a clear path to becoming a specialized, global legal AI serving all legal professionals worldwide.
